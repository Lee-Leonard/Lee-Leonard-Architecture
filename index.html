About Me

I am a recent Master of Architecture graduate from Queen’s University Belfast, graduating with a Distinction. My studies shaped my technical skills and design thinking, particularly around human-centric, sustainable, and public realm design.

Passionate about creating inclusive, environmentally responsible, and vibrant spaces, I am eager to continue developing as an architect while contributing to meaningful, research-driven design projects.
