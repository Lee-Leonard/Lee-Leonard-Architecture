Survey Overview

In October 2024, I participated in an academic study trip to Copenhagen with Queen's University Belfast (QUB) to explore the architectural legacy of Danish architect Hans Christian Hansen. A highlight of the trip was visiting Hansen's 'Skydebanehavens' building, now repurposed as 'Vesterbro Ungdomsgård,' a nursery school. This visit provided a rare opportunity to study Hansen's design principles in a modern context.By surveying and photographing key architectural details, I gained deeper insights into Hansen’s work and his enduring contributions to Copenhagen’s built environment. This hands-on experience was invaluable in understanding his architectural approach and its impact on the city.
